Some Famous Chess Players

Discover some of the greatest to ever play.

Magnus Carlsen

Norwegian chess grandmaster and World Chess Champion since 2013. Known for his versatile and dynamic playstyle.
He is considered to be the GOAT of chess.

Bobby Fischer

American chess prodigy and World Chess Champion (1972). Famous for his brilliance and contributions to chess theory.
Bobby Fischer stopped defending his chess title in 1975 after being unable to reach an agreement with FIDE, the international governing body for chess, over the match conditions.
As a result, Anatoly Karpov of the Soviet Union was named World Champion by default.

Garry Kasparov

Russian grandmaster, considered by many to be the greatest chess player of all time. Dominated the chess world for decades.
But in other peoples eyes, Magnus has overtook him to be the GOAT of chess.

Eugene Torre

Eugenio "Eugene" Torre is a Filipino chess grandmaster. In 1974, at 22 years old, he became the first Filipino and non-Soviet Asian to qualify for the title Grandmaster.
Torre did this by winning the silver medal in the 21st Chess Olympiad in Nice, France. He is also considered to be the strongest chess player the philippines has produced.
